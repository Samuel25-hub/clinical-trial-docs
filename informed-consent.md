# Participant Informed Consent Summary

## Study Title

A Study Evaluating HX-214 for Moderate Inflammatory Disease Symptoms

---

## Why Is This Study Being Done?

You are being invited to participate in a research study testing an investigational treatment called HX-214.

Researchers want to learn:
- whether the treatment improves symptoms
- how safe the treatment is
- how participants respond over time

---

## What Will Happen If I Join?

If you join the study, you may:

- attend scheduled clinic visits
- answer health-related questions
- receive study treatment or placebo
- complete medical tests and assessments
- report symptoms or side effects

The study is expected to last approximately 12 weeks.

---

## Are There Risks?

Some participants may experience side effects.

Possible risks may include:
- headache
- fatigue
- nausea
- injection site discomfort

There may also be risks that researchers do not yet know about.

Study staff will monitor participants closely during the study.

---

## Are There Benefits?

You may or may not receive direct medical benefit from participating.

Information learned from this study may help future patients and researchers better understand the condition being studied.

---

## Do I Have to Participate?

No.

Participation is completely voluntary.

You may choose not to participate or leave the study at any time without penalty or loss of regular medical care.

---

## Will My Information Be Protected?

Researchers will make reasonable efforts to protect your privacy and confidentiality.

Your personal information will not be shared outside the study except when required by law or research regulations.

---

## Who Can I Contact?

If you have questions about the study or experience side effects, contact the study research team.

---

## Consent Statement

By signing the official consent form, you confirm that:
- you understand the study information
- your questions have been answered
- you voluntarily agree to participate
